Our History

At Sri Bheemeswara Ambulance, our mission is to provide fast, reliable, and compassionate emergency medical services to the people of Srikakulam and surrounding regions. We are committed to saving lives by ensuring timely response and professional care during critical situations.

  • Rapid Response Ambulance Services
  • Advanced Medical Equipment
  • Skilled Paramedics & Drivers
  • Patient-Centered Approach
  • 24/7 Availability

Our vision is to be the most trusted and accessible ambulance service in Andhra Pradesh, setting the benchmark for emergency medical care. We aim to expand our reach, integrate modern technology, and strengthen community health support systems.

  • Building a Healthier Community
  • Expanding Services Across Andhra Pradesh
  • Integrating GPS & Smart Tracking
  • Continuous Training for Staff
  • Affordable & Transparent Services

Sri Bheemeswara Ambulance was established in Srikakulam over 30 years ago with a single vehicle dedicated to serving local communities. Over the decades, we have grown into a trusted name in emergency medical services, expanding our fleet and upgrading our facilities to meet modern healthcare needs.

  • Founded in 1995 in Srikakulam
  • Started with One Basic Ambulance
  • Expanded Fleet to Advanced Life Support Units
  • Recognized for Community Service
  • 30+ Years of Trusted Care

How to Get Ambulance Service

Step-01

Call Our Hotline

Call Sri Bheemeswara Ambulance anytime. Our team is available 24/7 to respond immediately to emergency and non-emergency requests.

Step-02

Share Patient Details

Provide basic details like patient condition, location, and type of service required (Emergency, ICU, or Mortuary Ambulance).

Step-03

Confirm Location

Share the exact pickup location and destination. Our driver will plan the fastest and safest route for timely arrival.

Step-04

Ambulance Arrives

Our fully equipped ambulance with trained staff reaches your location quickly and ensures safe patient transportation.

General FAQs

Common Questions About Our Ambulance Service

  • What types of emergencies do you handle?

    We handle medical emergencies such as accidents, heart attacks, breathing problems, trauma cases, and urgent hospital transfers.

  • What equipment is available in your ambulances?

    Our ambulances are equipped with oxygen cylinders, stretchers, first-aid kits, monitoring devices, and emergency medical support equipment depending on the ambulance type.

  • Do you provide hospital-to-hospital transfers?

    Yes, we provide safe inter-hospital and inter-city patient transfers with trained staff and continuous medical support when required.

  • Is your ambulance service available 24/7?

    Yes, Sri Bheemeswara Ambulance Services operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, including weekends and emergencies.
